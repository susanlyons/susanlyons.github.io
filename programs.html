Programming

Women in Measurement, Inc. hosts a number of initiatives aimed at advancing the careers of women in educational measurement, with particular focus on supporting women of color in our field.

Mentoring

Connect directly with women leaders in our field. Our mentoring program is designed to support women at all career stages of their careers.

Speaker Series

Presentations and panel discussions related to the intersection of women's issues, race, and educational measurement.

Networking Events

Join us at our annual networking reception to meet and connect with those who support the mission our organization.

The Women in Measurement, Inc. mentoring program seeks to create meaningful connections among women who are working in our field at any level. The need for mentoring does not end upon leaving graduate school or reaching mid-career status, which is why we match individuals at any point in their career with a mentor who is willing to connect, listen, and share.

Invited Session

Our inaugural Women in Measurement, Inc. speaker session will be held at the 2021 NCME Annual Meeting. Our session is intended to provide a venue to openly discuss the status of women in educational measurement, barriers to equal representation in leadership positions, and strategies and opportunities for overcoming those barriers.

All who are interested in supporting the mission of Women in Measurement, Inc. are invited to join us at our annual networking reception.
