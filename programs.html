Programming

Women in Measurement, Inc. hosts a number of initiatives aimed at advancing the careers of women in educational measurement, with particular focus on supporting women of color in our field.

Mentoring

Connect directly with women leaders in our field. Our mentoring program is designed to support women at all stages of their careers.

Speaker Series

Networking Events

Join us at our annual reception for informal conversation and networking with women in our field.

Stay informed about our upcoming mentoring sessions

The Women in Measurement, Inc. mentoring program seeks to create meaningful connections among women who are working in our field at any level. We host a series of interactive panel discussions facilitated by leaders in our field. These discussions are intended to provide information and support around relevant topics and create a space to foster conversation and build community.

Examples of topics include navigating the field as a woman of color, deciding between academia and industry, pathways to success within testing companies, and building your own business.

Invited Session

Our inaugural Women in Measurement, Inc. speaker session will be held at the 2021 NCME Annual Meeting. Our session is intended to provide a venue to openly discuss the status of women in educational measurement, barriers to equal representation in leadership positions, and strategies and opportunities for overcoming those barriers.
